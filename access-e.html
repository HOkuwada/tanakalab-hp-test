Access

From Nagoya Station to Tajimi Station

  • Take the JR Chuo Line (bound for Tajimi, Tokishi, or Nakatsugawa) and get off at Tajimi Station. (Approx. 40 minutes)

From Tajimi Station to the Institute (NIFS)

  • Tajimi Station, South Exit
  • Tono Tetsudo Bus (To-Tetsu Bus)
  • Take the Gakuen Toshi Line or the Toki-Tajimi Line, and get off at "National Institute for Fusion Science" (NIFS). (Please refer to here for the bus timetable.)

*Approx. 15 minutes by taxi.

For further details, please refer to the official Access Page of the National Institute for Fusion Science.
