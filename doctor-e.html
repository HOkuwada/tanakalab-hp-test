Postdoctoral Opportunities

Individuals who have obtained a Ph.D. degree can join our laboratory as a postdoctoral researcher. You can apply through the following two major fellowship programs.

Our laboratory is actively engaged in the development of advanced laser and microwave diagnostics, as well as plasma physics research using these systems. In addition to experimental work, we collaborate closely with the Numerical Simulation Research Project at NIFS to compare experimental data with neoclassical and turbulence simulations. We look forward to welcoming researchers specializing in these computational and theoretical fields as well. For specific research topics and proposals, please contact us individually for detailed discussions.

(1) JSPS Research Fellowship for Young Scientists (PD)

Application Period: Early April to early June every year

Tenure: 3 years

Please note that as a general rule, the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS) Postdoctoral Fellowship requires applicants to choose a host laboratory different from the one where they obtained their doctoral degree. For eligibility and further details, please refer to the official JSPS website:

https://www.jsps.go.jp/english/e-pd/index.html

(2) NIFS COE Research Fellow

Application Period: First round: June–July (Selection in September) / Second round: September–October (Selection in December)

Tenure: 2 years

For application guidelines and employment details, please visit the NIFS recruitment website:

https://www.nifs.ac.jp/en/jinji/index.html
